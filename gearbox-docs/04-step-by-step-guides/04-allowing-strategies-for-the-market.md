The strategy being a transaction bundle includes:

:

-
:

Rewards tokens generated by target collateral (e.g. CRV & CVX for Convex)
:

Intermediate tokens used for complex swaps (e.g. DAI -\> USDC -\> USDe -\> sUSDe -\> PT sUSDe requires having all the tokens listed as collaterals even though they can have 0 LT and limit)
