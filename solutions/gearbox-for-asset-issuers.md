# Gearbox for Asset Issuers
Gearbox for Asset Issuers
Gearbox empowers asset issuers to unlock scalable, capital-efficient leverage for their tokens — including RWAs, semi-liquid assets, LP positions and vaults.

With Gearbox, your users can open leveraged positions in one click, skip expensive DEX liquidity requirements, use any on-chain position as collateral, and benefit from a compliant, modular Credit Account architecture.

This transforms your token ecosystem: deeper liquidity, higher TVL, more active strategies — all without needing to build leverage infrastructure yourself.
