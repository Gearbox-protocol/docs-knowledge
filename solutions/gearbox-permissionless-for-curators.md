# Gearbox Permissionless for Curators

## Overview

Gearbox lets fintech companies, asset managers, structured product desks, and sophisticated DeFi operators operate a capital-efficient lending business without building a protocol, managing liquidity, or dealing with the operational and regulatory burden of handling user funds.

Define the market, its risk parameters, collateral mix, and pricing logic and Gearbox executes everything automatically and securely under the hood.

A Curator on Gearbox effectively becomes the owner of a lending instance: a standalone vertical that attracts deposits from passive liquidity providers and offers leverage or borrowing opportunities to users.

## No Active Liquidity Management Needed

On Gearbox, Curators control risk settings such as LTVs, collateral types, and asset-specific limits, but never the funds themselves. This minimizes transaction overhead and provides users with a seamless, capital-efficient experience.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Reducing compliance overhead:** By eliminating the need for manual liquidity management, Curators can avoid activities that in many jurisdictions could be classified as financial intermediation (managing liquidity directly).
{% endhint %}

## No-Slippage, No-Price-Impact Leverage UX

Execution stays where the capital is, allowing superior UX and capital-efficiency for borrowers.

Direct integration of vaults, RWAs, LSTs, and other yield strategies allows deposits and redemptions to be executed natively with leverage inside Credit Accounts. This capability is unique to Gearbox.

## Novel Safety-first mechanisms

Oracles that combine multiple price sources, paired with policies to prevent bad debt, offer stronger protection for LPs while maintaining capital efficiency for leverage users.

## Built to Scale, Multichain by Default

From storing contract bytecode in a custom “on-chain GitHub” to self-hosted operational tools like a Multisig UI, built-in liquidity aggregation, and broad DeFi collateral support, the infrastructure stack is designed to accelerate product and ecosystem growth while ensuring security is enforced programmatically.

## Security & Compliance Resilience

Security is foundational at Gearbox. Over four years of continuous mainnet operation, the protocol has incurred zero bad debt and suffered no security exploits, placing it among the most battle-tested lending protocols in DeFi.

Backed by more than $3 million invested in audits, formal verification, and real-time monitoring, Gearbox pairs strong technical resilience with built-in compliance flexibility, allowing curators to deploy KYC-gated or permissioned markets, restrict borrower and lender access, and even operate single-borrower instances when regulatory or operational requirements demand it.
