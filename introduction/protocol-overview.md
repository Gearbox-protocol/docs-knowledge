# Permissionless Platform for Onchain Credit

Gearbox is a modular credit platform that anyone can plug into to add onchain borrowing and leverage with a production-ready stack shaped by years of mainnet learnings so teams stay focused on business, not tech.

 Lenders, wallets, apps, and asset teams can launch curated credit lines that stay composable across DeFi so users keep control of their positions. 

# What Gearbox Enables
## Composable onchain credit
Rather than sealing collateral and debt into protocol-global pools, Gearbox lets each user steer a dedicated smart contract account so their portfolio stays intact and portable. Credit flows stay fluid across partners and chains, making leverage, trading, and portfolio management feel like one simple experience instead of hopping between siloed venues.

## Diversified yield for liquidity providers
Single-asset pools fund curated credit books, so lenders earn from active leverage demand across trading, farming, and RWA borrowers. Loans overcollateralization is supported by curated loan-to-value, dual-oracle pricing, liquidations, and no curator custody over deposits, keeping depositor safety front and center.

## A modular platform for building credit products
Gearbox provides a credit stack so wallets, RWA issuers, and funds can launch branded lending programs without building core infrastructure. Partners configure access, integrations, and risk controls to fit their mandate while users interact through familiar, native-feeling flows. The platform spans chains and adapters to shorten time-to-market while keeping business-decisions, distribution and compliance in partner hands.
